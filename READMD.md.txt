## Online Recipe Organizer User Manual

### Adding a Recipe:
1. Click on the "Add Recipe" button.
2. Fill in the recipe details in the form.
3. Click "Save Recipe" to add the recipe to your collection.

### Editing a Recipe:
1. Navigate to the recipe you want to edit.
2. Click on the "Edit" button.
3. Modify the recipe details in the form.
4. Click "Save" to update the recipe.

### Deleting a Recipe:
1. Navigate to the recipe you want to delete.
2. Click on the "Delete" button.
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
